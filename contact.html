Contact Pangeo¶

Discourse¶

Our primary forum for communication is our Discourse Forum. This is the best place to ask general questions about the project or get involved in community activities.

Github¶

We try to conduct all technical communication via GitHub. The repo pangeo-data/pangeo is a general place to discuss technical issues. The pangeo-data GitHub org contains dozens of other inter-related projects.

Other Media¶

Pangeo’s online presence also includes a:

  • Medium Blog: https://medium.com/pangeo

  • Twitter Account: https://twitter.com/pangeo_data

If wish to contact Pangeo via a non-public channel, you may email the following Pangeo collaborators:

  • Ryan Abernathey (Columbia / LDEO): rpa@ldeo.columbia.edu

  • Joe Hamman (NCAR): jhamman@ucar.edu
